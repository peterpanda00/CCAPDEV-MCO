Our Story

Our Background

Park Avenue Guesthouse 2443 is situated within a serene compound near Libertad, Pasay. The distinguished house, originally built in the 1920s, has recently undergone a meticulous renovation to transform into an exquisite guesthouse. Since its opening in February 2017, the guesthouse has attracted attention from both local and international travelers in search of a cozy and distinctive place to stay for a night or more. Particularly popular among foreign guests, Park Avenue Guesthouse 2443 stands out as the only known guesthouse in the area, offering a unique experience for visitors seeking a memorable stay.

Our Vision

At our esteemed guesthouse, we are driven by a clear vision - to be the premier destination for travelers seeking an authentic Philippine experience. Our commitment lies in preserving the historical relics and rich cultural heritage that define our nation, while delivering exceptional hospitality and personalized service. With genuine warmth and a friendly approach, our ultimate goal is to showcase the captivating beauty and diverse wonders of our country through our unique accommodations and locally-inspired offerings. As we embark on this journey, we aspire to become a leading force in sustainable tourism, making a positive impact on both the environment and the communities we have the privilege to serve. We extend a warm invitation to join us on this remarkable voyage.
