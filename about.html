Our Story

Our Background

Located within a serene compound near Libertad, Pasay, Park Avenue Guesthouse 2443 holds a rich history dating back to its original construction in the 1920s. After undergoing a recent renovation, the former house has been transformed into a charming guesthouse. Since its opening in February 2017, it has attracted the attention of both local and foreign nationals seeking a cozy and distinctive accommodation option for a night or an extended stay.

Park Avenue Guesthouse 2443 has gained particular popularity among foreign guests, thanks to its status as the sole known guesthouse in the area. Its unique offerings and warm hospitality have earned it a reputation as a sought-after destination for travelers seeking a memorable experience. With its convenient location and the attention to detail in its renovations, the guesthouse provides a welcoming and inviting atmosphere for guests to enjoy a peaceful and comfortable stay.

Our History

Park Avenue Guesthouse 2443, formerly known as the Trinidad household, holds a rich history dating back to its original construction in the 1920s. Originally located in Malate, Manila, the house was built by Mr. Wenceslao Trinidad, one of the first Filipino Bureau of Internal Revenue collectors.

However, due to the upheaval of World War II, the Trinidad house was dismantled and relocated to its present site in Pasay City. Considered a historical house within the compound, it stands as one of the oldest houses in Manila.

In 2015, the Trinidad house underwent a change of ownership and commenced its restoration to recapture its traditional charm, offering a serene haven for guests seeking a peaceful neighborhood atmosphere. With its convenient proximity of 6.6 kilometers to the airport and its central location in the bustling city of Manila, Park Avenue Guesthouse 2443 provides an ideal base for exploring the captivating attractions in the surrounding area.

Our Vision

At our esteemed guesthouse, we are driven by a clear vision - to be the premier destination for travelers seeking an authentic Philippine experience. Our commitment lies in preserving the historical relics and rich cultural heritage that define our nation, while delivering exceptional hospitality and personalized service.

With genuine warmth and a friendly approach, our ultimate goal is to showcase the captivating beauty and diverse wonders of our country through our unique accommodations and locally-inspired offerings.

As we embark on this journey, we aspire to become a leading force in sustainable tourism, making a positive impact on both the environment and the communities we have the privilege to serve. We extend a warm invitation to join us on this remarkable voyage.

At our guesthouse, we are deeply committed to creating a positive impact on society and the environment through our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) program. We prioritize sustainability by implementing eco-friendly practices, such as waste reduction, energy and water conservation, and responsible sourcing. Preserving the history, relics, and culture of our community is of utmost importance to us.

In addition to our environmental efforts, we actively support local initiatives focused on education, health, and social welfare. By giving back to our community, we strive to make a lasting and positive impact on the lives of those in need. Our comprehensive CSR program reflects our unwavering dedication to preserving the environment, supporting the community, and ensuring a sustainable future for generations to come.
